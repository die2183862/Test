    Policy Page

    Site Purpose Statement

    At Before It Melts, our mission is to inform, educate, and empower individuals and communities about climate change and environmental sustainability. We provide accessible, accurate, and up-to-date information, including scientific data, educational resources, and actionable insights. Our goal is to foster a deeper understanding of environmental challenges and promote meaningful solutions through collaboration, innovation, and public engagement.
    We are committed to delivering trustworthy content that supports lifelong learning, inspires environmental stewardship, and encourages individual and collective action to address the climate crisis.

    Accessibility Statement

    Before It Melts is dedicated to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We strive to continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to meet or exceed Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

    Our efforts include:

    • Designing content with clear structure and readability in mind.
    • Providing alternative text for all meaningful images.
    • Ensuring navigation is accessible by keyboard and assistive technologies.
    • Regularly testing our site for usability across a variety of devices and screen readers.

    If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us here. We value your feedback and are committed to making the site as inclusive as possible.
